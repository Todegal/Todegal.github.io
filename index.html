Niall Townley

NIALL TOWNLEY is a London based pianist and accompanist specialising in song and chamber repertoire. He is currently pursuing a Masters in Collaborative Piano at Guildhall School of Music, where he studies with Andrew West.


He has distinguished himself as a song accompanist, participating in projects run by Iain Burnside, Eugene Asti, and Alison Wells. In 2025 Niall took part in the Ludlow English Song Festival's Young Artist masterclass given by Dame Anne Murray.


In 2026, Niall was selected for Graham Johnson's Song Guild, and was awarded the Paul Hamburger prize for best pianist after the recital. In the same month Niall won the Franz Schubert Institute prize at Guildhall and was awarded a full scholarship to their summer programme.
